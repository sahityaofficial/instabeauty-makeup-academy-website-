About Us

Instabeauty Makeup Academy is a premier beauty and makeup training institute dedicated to empowering students with modern, practical, and industry-ready skills. Based in Indore, we are known for our top-quality education, expert trainers, and a learning environment where creativity and confidence grow together. Our mission is to transform passionate learners into skilled, professional makeup artists ready to shine in the beauty and fashion industry.

With state-of-the-art facilities, personalized mentorship, and small batch sizes, we ensure focused learning and hands-on experience. Students work on live models, gain portfolio-building opportunities, and receive complete career guidance. At Instabeauty, we believe every artist has potential—our goal is to help you discover it and build a successful future.
